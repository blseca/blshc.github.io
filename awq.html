Academic WorldQuest

AWQ(Academic WorldQuest) is a foreign affairs and current events competition for High Schoolers. We compete to qualify to Nationals in DC, having consecutively championed the State tournament 2 years in a row, and have sent teams to the all-expense paid national competition. With our interactive practice sessions, Middle schoolers also have a great opportunity to learn about Foreign Affairs, and eventually join the team. Sending multiple teams to states, we welcome all to participate.
